About
me

My name is Dennis Timmers, currently I study interactive media design at Grafisch lyceum Rotterdam.
In this study we learn how to develop websites, apps and do a lot of concepting as well as graphic design.
My main focus lies on front-end, I have a good understanding of HTML, SASS and jQuery.
I also completed the study signmaking and outdoor advertising.
This is where I gained a lot of knowledge in Photoshop, Illustrator and Indesign.

Currently I work as graphic designer for Het Vrienden Café in Zoetermeer.
I keep busy with event advertising, both print and digital as well as menu cards and signing.
